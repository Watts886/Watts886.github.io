Frequently asked

How often does the leaderboard update?

The connected Google Sheet refreshes automatically every minute by default.

Can features be hidden before Oscar Night?

Yes. Awards, rules, bonus points and Oscar Night elements each have developer-only toggles in script.js.

Why are mobile standings cards used?

Cards keep player names and score totals readable without forcing horizontal table scrolling.
